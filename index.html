About Me


BIO

I started running in 2000 and cycling in 2001. Within a couple of years I did my first marathon, then triathlon, then started running ultra marathons. I'm oringinally from Chicago, but now live in Jamestown, CO with my partner Melissa, our 3 years old daughter Emilia and our french bulldog.

BLOG

This blog is about my personal experiences with training and racing triathlons, ironmans, marathons, ultramarathons, bike rides and other shorter races. You will find race reports with course descriptions as well as training reports. I hope blog this inspires you to push your own limits.


BOOK

This book will emcompass my training leading up to my attempt of a Deca Ironman in November 2018 and my journey doing the race. The race format is one triathlon per day for 10 consecutive days. No American has completed this format and I hope to be the first.
