About Me


Bio

I started running in 2000 and cycling in 2001.I have always had an active lifestyle of biking, running and hiking, but only in the last few years have I taken on endurance racing and triathlons. I've competed in many ultra marathons and triathlons and have done charity runs such as running across Illinois. I love the training and the challenges that go with extremely difficult races. I am 47 years old and live in Jamestown, Colorado with my partner, Melissa, our 3 year old daughter Emilia and our French Bulldog. Melissa is also an endurance athlete and participates in ultra marathons... 200 mile races are her sweet spot. We both love pushing our limits and love all the adventures we go on as a family.
